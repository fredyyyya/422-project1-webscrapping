        General Info
        Established in 1994, Extreme Pizza is a chain of fast food restaurants. The eatery provides various delicacies to its guests. The restaurant serves menu items, salads, subs, pizzas and calzones. It provides a range of sauces, such as tomato, pesto, black bean/salsa and hummus. Extreme Pizza has also been ranked on the INC 500's Fastest Growing Companies for three consecutive years. It is a recipient of 'Best Pizza award' in San Francisco, the East Bay and the Bay Area .
